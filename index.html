About

Who We Are

Geospace Instrument Services is a trusted provider of advanced GNSS solutions, handheld laser scanners, drones, underground detection systems, hydrographic survey equipment, and other professional geomatics and surveying technologies. We deliver reliable, precise, and high-performance instruments to industries where accuracy is critical.

We proudly support surveyors, engineers, construction teams, agriculture specialists, and GIS professionals with quality equipment backed by dependable technical expertise and responsive service.

Driven by accuracy, efficiency, and innovation, we are committed to helping our customers work smarter, faster, and with confidence. From product consultation and system integration to training and after-sales support, Geospace partners with every client to ensure the highest standards of performance, reliability, and project success.
