About Us

Geospace Instrument Services is a trusted provider of GNSS handhelds, receivers, and survey instruments. Our mission is to empower professionals in surveying, construction, agriculture, and GIS with reliable and accurate solutions.

  • R60 Android Data Collector
  • Handheld Series (P8H, P8, P6H, P6)
